About Me


Why 'Panda'

Hi, I'm Chongdan Pan from Shanghai, China. People also know me as Panda, not only because it sounds like my Chinese name but also because it symbolizes Chinese culture. Pandas are globally beloved for their adorable appearance, serving as a wonderful symbol that bridges the gap between Chinese culture and worldwide admiration. The name 'Panda' embodies the qualities of peacefulness and harmony that I aspire to embrace. Like these adorable creatures, I strive to bring joy and harmony to the lives of thosearound me.

Childhood in Huangpu

Huangpu District, the place where I spent my first 18 years, holds the essence of Shanghai, both geographically and spiritually. Its inclusive and diverse culture cultivated a wide range of interests within me. Every cherished memory is etched in my heart, from the exhilaration of rushing out of the classroom to play basketball, to the countless hours spent tinkering with robots in the lab. I would also stroll along the enchanting Huangpu River, immersing myself in the harmonious fusion of modernity and tradition.

Journey in Quantitative Finance

After graduating from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, I joined Probquant Investment, a high frequency trading start-up. There, I devoted myself to developing trading and research systems and took immense pride in our efforts to enhance market efficiency.
During my internship at Jump Trading, their relentless scientific approach left a deep impression on me. Now, I eagerly look forward to embracing new challenges at the D.E. Shaw Group.
